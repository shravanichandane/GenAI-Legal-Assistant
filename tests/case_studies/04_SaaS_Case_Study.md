# LegalSight Case Study: SaaS Agreement

## 1. Context
Evaluation of a standard SaaS platform subscription agreement, analyzing data ownership and usage rights claimed by the SaaS provider.

## 2. Mock Clause
**Clause ID:** `clause_saas_027`
> "Provider retains all rights to aggregated and anonymized data generated by Customer's use of the Service. Provider may use this data to improve the Service and for any other commercial purposes."

## 3. Playbook Violation
**Playbook Rule ID:** `rule_saas_data_usage`
**Rule Description:** SaaS providers may use aggregated/anonymized data for service improvement, but explicitly not for "any other commercial purposes" (like selling data to third parties).

## 4. Risk Assessment
### Risk Score: MEDIUM

**Risk Explanation:**
- **Factor**: Vague language ("any other commercial purposes") permits unrestricted data monetization.
- **Factor**: Potential conflict with internal data privacy policies regarding customer data derivations.
- **Recommended Mitigation**: Delete the phrase "and for any other commercial purposes." Restrict data usage explicitly to "improving the core service provided to the Customer."
